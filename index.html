The AI Productivity vs. Security Dilemma

Companies face an impossible choice: embrace AI productivity or maintain data security.

Employees are using AI tools everywhere—ChatGPT, Gemini, coding assistants, browser extensions— often pasting in sensitive documents without realizing the compliance implications.

Companies in regulated industries can't answer critical questions: "What data has left our walls, through which AI tools, and under what policies?"

Security leaders are told to both encourage AI usage for productivity gains and prevent any data leaks— an impossible balance with current tools that only see part of the picture.

Shadow AI Usage

Unsanctioned AI tools on personal devices and unapproved platforms

Data Leakage Risk

Sensitive information shared with third-party AI models without oversight

Compliance Blindness

No audit trail for AI interactions across enterprise applications

Regulatory Pressure

Increasing expectations for AI governance and data residency controls
