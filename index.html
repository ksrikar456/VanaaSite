Why Vanaa Is Different

Current solutions only solve part of the AI governance puzzle. Vanaa provides complete endpoint control.

Governance Platforms

What they do: Operate at policy and documentation level with frameworks and inventories.

What they miss: No control over real-time employee behavior on actual devices and applications.

Network & GenAI DLP

What they do: Monitor data in transit or specific browsers for sensitive information.

What they miss: Limited visibility into desktop apps, IDEs, and non-browser tools. Cannot distinguish helpful AI usage from harmful leakage.

Embedded Vendor Assistants

What they do: Provide AI capabilities within a single ecosystem or productivity suite.

What they miss: Cannot govern how employees use competing tools or third-party AI models outside their ecosystem.

Vanaa's Approach

Endpoint-based: Complete visibility into user workflows across all applications and AI interactions.

Model-neutral: Supports multiple AI providers without vendor lock-in, giving organizations choice and flexibility.

Single control point: One unified layer that bridges policy governance with actual employee AI usage behavior.
